# Evidence Pattern: Circle Time Participation

## Context
Children participating in whole-group gatherings (morning meeting, read-aloud, calendar, discussions).

## Observable Behaviors (Signals)

| Indicator | Behaviors |
|-----------|-----------|
| `indicator.self_regulation.attention` | Sitting in designated spot, looking at speaker, raising hand, staying engaged. |
| `indicator.self_regulation.emotions` | Managing excitement, waiting patiently, coping with not being chosen. |
| `indicator.belonging.community` | Contributing ideas, listening to peers, celebrating others' contributions. |

## Teacher Moves (Prompts)
- "I noticed you waited patiently for your turn. That was helpful."
- "What strategy did you use to stay focused?"
- "How did it feel when your friend shared that idea?"

## Sample Observation Note (Template)
> At circle time, {child} {action} during [activity]. {pronoun_subject} showed {skill} by {evidence}. This is {growth_note} compared to earlier in the year.
