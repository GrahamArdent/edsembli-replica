# Evidence Pattern: Art Creation Process

## Context
Children engaged in open-ended art activities (painting, collage, sculpture, drawing).

## Observable Behaviors (Signals)

| Indicator | Behaviors |
|-----------|-----------|
| `indicator.problem_solving.creativity` | Making original choices, combining materials in new ways, expressing ideas visually. |
| `indicator.problem_solving.innovation` | Problem-solving when materials don't work as expected, revising and iterating. |

## Teacher Moves (Prompts)
- "Tell me about your artwork."
- "What made you choose those colours/materials?"
- "What will you try next time?"

## Sample Observation Note (Template)
> {child} created [artwork type] using [materials]. {pronoun_subject} demonstrated creativity by {evidence}. When {challenge} arose, {pronoun_subject} solved it by {solution}.
