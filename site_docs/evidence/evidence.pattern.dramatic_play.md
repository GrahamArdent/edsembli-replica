# Evidence Pattern: Dramatic Play Centre

## Context
Children engaged in role-playing scenarios (house, restaurant, hospital, etc.) in the dramatic play area.

## Observable Behaviors (Signals)

| Indicator | Behaviors |
|-----------|-----------|
| `indicator.belonging.relationships` | Negotiating roles, inviting others to play, sharing props, resolving conflicts verbally. |
| `indicator.belonging.identity` | Trying on different roles, expressing preferences, representing family/cultural experiences. |

## Teacher Moves (Prompts)
- "I noticed you're the chef today. What's on the menu?"
- "How did you decide who would be the doctor?"
- "What does your character need to do next?"

## Sample Observation Note (Template)
> {child} chose the role of [role] in dramatic play today. {pronoun_subject} demonstrated [skill] by [specific action]. When [situation arose], {pronoun_subject} responded by [response].
