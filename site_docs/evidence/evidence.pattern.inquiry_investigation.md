# Evidence Pattern: Inquiry Investigation

## Context
Children exploring questions through hands-on investigation (sink/float, magnets, plants, shadows).

## Observable Behaviors (Signals)

| Indicator | Behaviors |
|-----------|-----------|
| `indicator.problem_solving.inquiry` | Asking questions, making predictions, testing ideas, observing closely, recording findings. |
| `indicator.problem_solving.innovation` | Designing experiments, modifying approach based on results, connecting ideas. |

## Teacher Moves (Prompts)
- "What do you wonder about this?"
- "What do you think will happen? Why?"
- "What did you discover? How do you know?"

## Sample Observation Note (Template)
> {child} investigated [topic/question] by {method}. {pronoun_subject} predicted that {prediction} and discovered that {finding}. {pronoun_subject} showed curiosity by {evidence}.
