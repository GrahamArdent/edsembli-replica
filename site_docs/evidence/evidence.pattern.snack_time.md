# Evidence Pattern: Snack Time Routine

## Context
Children participating in the daily snack routine (handwashing, serving, eating, cleanup).

## Observable Behaviors (Signals)

| Indicator | Behaviors |
|-----------|-----------|
| `indicator.self_regulation.health` | Washing hands independently, making healthy choices, using utensils appropriately. |
| `indicator.self_regulation.attention` | Following multi-step routines, waiting turn, staying seated during eating. |

## Teacher Moves (Prompts)
- "What do we do first before we eat?"
- "How do you know when you're finished?"
- "I see you remembered all the steps today."

## Sample Observation Note (Template)
> During snack time, {child} demonstrated {skill} by {evidence}. {pronoun_subject} {action} without reminders, showing growth in {area}.
