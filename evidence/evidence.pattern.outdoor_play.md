# Evidence Pattern: Outdoor Gross Motor Play

## Context
Children engaged in physical play outdoors (climbing, running, bike riding, ball games).

## Observable Behaviors (Signals)

| Indicator | Behaviors |
|-----------|-----------|
| `indicator.self_regulation.health` | Making safe choices, assessing risk, dressing appropriately, knowing limits. |
| `indicator.problem_solving.inquiry` | Testing physical capabilities, trying new challenges, persisting through difficulty. |

## Teacher Moves (Prompts)
- "What made you decide to try that?"
- "How did you know it was safe to climb that high?"
- "What will you try differently next time?"

## Sample Observation Note (Template)
> During outdoor play, {child} chose to [activity]. {pronoun_subject} demonstrated {skill} when {evidence}. {pronoun_subject} showed awareness of safety by {safety_action}.
