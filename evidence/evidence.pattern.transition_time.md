# Evidence Pattern: Transition Times

## Context
Children moving between activities or spaces (cleanup, lining up, changing activities, arrival/dismissal).

## Observable Behaviors (Signals)

| Indicator | Behaviors |
|-----------|-----------|
| `indicator.self_regulation.attention` | Following transition signals, completing cleanup tasks, moving purposefully. |
| `indicator.self_regulation.emotions` | Managing feelings about ending preferred activities, coping with change. |

## Teacher Moves (Prompts)
- "What do you need to do to be ready?"
- "I can see it's hard to stop playing. What could help?"
- "You followed all the steps without a reminder today!"

## Sample Observation Note (Template)
> During the transition from [activity A] to [activity B], {child} {behaviour}. {pronoun_subject} demonstrated {skill} by {evidence}. This shows growth because {comparison}.
