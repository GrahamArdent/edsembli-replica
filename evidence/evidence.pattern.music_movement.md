# Evidence Pattern: Music and Movement

## Context
Children participating in songs, dances, and musical activities (action songs, freeze dance, instruments).

## Observable Behaviors (Signals)

| Indicator | Behaviors |
|-----------|-----------|
| `indicator.belonging.community` | Participating in group activities, following along, contributing to shared experience. |
| `indicator.belonging.identity` | Expressing self through movement, sharing cultural songs/dances, showing preferences. |
| `indicator.self_regulation.emotions` | Using music to regulate mood, transitioning smoothly with musical cues. |

## Teacher Moves (Prompts)
- "How does this music make you feel?"
- "Can you show me a different way to move?"
- "Do you know a song like this from home?"

> During music and movement, {child} {participation_level}. {pronoun_subject} demonstrated {skill} by {evidence}. {pronoun_subject} showed {trait} when {specific_moment}.
