# Evidence Pattern: Writing Centre Exploration

## Context
Children engaged in purposeful mark-making and writing activities (lists, labels, stories, letters).

## Observable Behaviors (Signals)

| Indicator | Behaviors |
|-----------|-----------|
| `indicator.literacy_math.writing` | Using letter-like forms, copying environmental print, sound-spelling, drawing to communicate. |
| `indicator.literacy_math.oral_language` | Explaining their writing, reading back their work, asking for spelling help. |

## Teacher Moves (Prompts)
- "Tell me about your writing."
- "What sounds do you hear in that word?"
- "Who is this message for?"

## Sample Observation Note (Template)
> At the writing centre, {child} created a [type of writing] about [topic]. {pronoun_subject} demonstrated {skill} by {evidence}. When asked to read it back, {pronoun_subject} {reading_behaviour}.
