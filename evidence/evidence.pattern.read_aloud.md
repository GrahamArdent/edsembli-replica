# Evidence Pattern: Read-Aloud Engagement

## Context
Children participating in interactive read-aloud sessions (predicting, questioning, connecting).

## Observable Behaviors (Signals)

| Indicator | Behaviors |
|-----------|-----------|
| `indicator.literacy_math.reading` | Making predictions, noticing print features, retelling story elements. |
| `indicator.literacy_math.oral_language` | Asking questions, making connections, using new vocabulary. |

## Teacher Moves (Prompts)
- "What do you think will happen next? Why?"
- "Does this remind you of anything?"
- "What does that word mean? How do you know?"

## Sample Observation Note (Template)
> During read-aloud of [book title], {child} {engagement_action}. {pronoun_subject} demonstrated comprehension by {evidence}. {pronoun_subject} used the vocabulary word "{word}" when {context}.
